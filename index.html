About JT Hair

I’m Johnny Thai, a stylist with over 15 years of experience working with both men and women. I specialize in dry cuts that save you time without compromising style.

Besides cutting, I’m also known for my balayage coloring technique. When your hair is in good condition, and expectations are realistic, balayage adds natural color and dimension to dull hair.

I was unable to operate for a while due to an unexpected open heart surgery, but now I’m back better than ever.

Hair isn’t just a job for me, it’s a passion that grows stronger every day. Thanks to social media, I’m always learning new techniques to bring you the best look.
